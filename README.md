cleaning raw transaction data, engineering time-based features, training and evaluating forecasting models, and producing a 12-week forward forecast with business-friendly visuals.
What's in here:
1. Load & inspect the raw data
2. Clean the data (real data-quality issues found and fixed)
3. Explore trend & seasonality
4. Engineer time-based features
5. Train & evaluate forecasting models
6. Forecast the next 12 weeks
7. Business takeaways
